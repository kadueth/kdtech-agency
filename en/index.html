FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build my website?+
It depends on the plan: Landing Pages are ready in 5 days, Professional websites in 10 days, and Business plans with custom systems in up to 21 business days. Timelines start from contract signing and content receipt.
Do I need to organise my own domain and hosting?+
Not at all! We handle everything for you. The first year of domain and hosting is included in all plans. After that, you only pay the annual renewal (typically around A$80/year).
Will I be able to update my website myself?+
Yes! Every website comes with a simple admin panel. We also provide free training and video tutorials so you can make basic updates without relying on us.
Will my website show up on Google?+
All our websites are built with SEO best practices (content, speed, meta tags, sitemap). For even better results, we recommend our monthly maintenance plan with ongoing SEO.
How does payment work?+
We accept bank transfer, credit card and PayPal. We charge 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For more complex systems, we can split into 3 milestones.
What if I'm not happy with the result?+
You review and approve every stage before we proceed. We include up to 3 rounds of design revisions at no extra cost. Your site only goes live when you're 100% happy.
